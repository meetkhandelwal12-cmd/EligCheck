About EligCheck

EligCheck is an independent platform built to help Indian students and aspirants understand which government exams they are eligible for based on age, qualification and category.

This website is created for guidance purposes only. Always refer to official notifications for final eligibility details.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does category give age relaxation?

Yes. OBC candidates usually get 3 years and SC/ST candidates get 5 years age relaxation.

Is EligCheck an official government website?

No. EligCheck is an independent, student-created platform.

Are the results 100% accurate?

The results are indicative. Always check official exam notifications.
